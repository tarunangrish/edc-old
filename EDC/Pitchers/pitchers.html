About Pitchers

EDC Cell Thapar University conducted an event named Pitchers. It was to let the students an insight into the business world.
The chief guest for the day was Mr Sakil chawla, the CEO of orange twig.
Motive: In today’s world when every second person is inclined towards entrepreneurship, Pitchers acted as a first step towards helping them set up their target. Pitching is the most important part of any startup. It doesn’t matter how big or small your idea is, if you know how to pitch it in 3 minutes, you get the gold in your pocket.

There were about 28 teams with seven members each and they were divided into two slots. All the teams were allotted an already existing start up and given certain posts in the company like CMO ,CTO ,CTO, Investors, R&D and CFO. Then they were given time to research on their startups and after that they had to pitch their startup in front of the panel of investors.
The students were full of enthusiasm as they came on stage to display their strategies and manipulate the panel. It was followed by a round of questions from the jury.
