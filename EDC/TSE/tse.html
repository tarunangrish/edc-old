About The Startup Expo

North India's largest exposition for startup ecosystem, The Startup Expo bring together the most innovative and disruptive startups, along with C level executives from some of the top corporations, government agencies, incubators, investors, HNIs, VC funds, students and technology companies from across the country. The Startup Expo was an intellectual bonanza to celebrate innovation dedicated to advancing technology to serve humanity better. It featured a 2-day exhibition and conference sessions by thought leaders, experienced entrepreneurs, investors and key policy makers.

Startups from all over North India came to contribute their creativity towards the legit startup culture. 22 of the most innovative startups with a team of 4-5 members each pitched their ideas with the aim to join the ends of the future with present.

The event was organized on 12 th Feburuary,2017 in Thapar University,Patiala.


The esteem panel of Jury and Investors included :-

  • CVS Sehgal - Chief GM, C and C Constructions Ltd
  • Kanwar Nihal Singh Gill - CEO (Company Name - X 3D Studio Pvt. Ltd.)
  • Captain Jagdeep Kahlon - Chief Officer at Mitsui OSK Lines Maritime (I) Pvt. Ltd.
  • Heena Singal - Founding Member, E9 News
  • Karminder Ghuman - Founder, Venture Labs.
  • Karun Verma - Vice President, EDC.
  • MD Singh - President, EDC.
  • Investors From Punjab Angel Network.
