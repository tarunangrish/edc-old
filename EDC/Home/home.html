Entrepreneurship Development Cell, Thapar University commonly known as EDC, has been established with the objective of creating, fostering and promoting the spirit of entrepreneurship among the youth of the nation. Created in the early months of 2014, the cell has already taken rapid strides in cultivating entrepreneurial temper and has succeeded in carving a niche for itself.

EDC aims at developing the spirit of entrepreneurship among the students of Thapar University. It not only wants to ignite the minds but also to nurture the ideas of students . EDC is committed to build a strong platform for budding entrepreneurs as a career, as a path to success, as a journey of wisdom.

Our Vision

Our vision is to develop entrepreneurial ecosystem of India by bringing the leading edge ideas to the forefront and converting them into business thus promoting the economy of the country and ultimately improving the quality of life.



Our Aim

Our aim is to bridge the gap between the ideas and their implementation and give wings to the ideas nesting in the minds of the students. Imagination dies a dreadful death as we grow but here at EDC Thapar, we nurture the imagination of young budding entrepreneurs by providing a platform to present their seminal ideas. We strive to make students accessible with resources and provide them an easy link to venture capital firms, successful entrepreneurs, executive professionals and corporates so that they can watch their dream become reality.
