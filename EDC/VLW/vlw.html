About Venture Lab Weekend

Venture Lab is an event to spread the entrepreneurial mindset to empower students with the tools needed to become the next generation of innovators and change makers.

The 3 day workshop will help the students understand how to inculcate an idea and make a profitable, marketable, feasible business out of it in the current orthodox environment . They’ll be prepared to talk to investors, customers and the benefits of collaboration and the art of sustaining it in high-pressure situations.

Venture Lab weekend was the major attraction among the events organized by EDC. It provided ample opportunity to promising ventures. It was a 3 day workshop held during 2nd - 4th September in Thapar University, it presented opportunities to learn basics of Entrepreneurship and Ventures.

Participants were taught things like how to pitch their ideas, value proposition and many more things which are essential for a startup. Every student was dealt personally by our facilitator and mentors which guided them to carve their own skills and also allowed them to work in teams in order to give them a check of the collaborative environment. With the basics in hand, the teams were then introduced with the concept of Business Model Canvas and were told to submit their ideas in the form of BMC. Giving the event a sense of competition as experienced in the real-time scenario, the teams with the best BMCs were awarded.
